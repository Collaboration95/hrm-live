# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in HRM Live to be welcoming, respectful, and free from
harassment. Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to act in good
faith and make room for people with different backgrounds and experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and open to feedback.
- Discuss code and ideas without personal attacks, discrimination, threats, or
  harassment.
- Respect privacy: do not post another person's health data, BLE identifiers,
  credentials, or private correspondence.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and help correct them.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, hate speech, intimidation, sexualized content or attention,
doxxing, deliberate disruption, and repeated bad-faith conduct are not
acceptable.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub
profile. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers
may remove content, warn participants, restrict participation, or take other
proportionate action to protect the community.

This policy applies to project spaces and to public behavior that materially
affects the project community.
